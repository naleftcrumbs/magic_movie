how to read the statistics

Predicted rating

The app’s prediction for your own rating based on your responses. It combines overall movie quality from MovieLens data, your questionnaire answers, and learned rating patterns across MovieLens users.

rating = bayes movie mean + residual prediction

95% prediction interval

A likely range for your rating if the predicted rating is off. The narrower the interval, the more certain it is :D

rating ± 1.96 × prediction SE

Uncertainty level

This measures how reliable a recommendation is, based on ratings from our MovieLens dataset. If reliability is higher, more users would be likely to agree with it.

0.55 × variance + 0.45 × count uncertainty

Final recommendation score

A ranking metric that accounts for how well you may like the movie, how certain the suggestion is, and whether you prefer rare or widely rated movies.

score = rating - uncertainty penalty + match bonuses
